Myths, laws, powers

  • The 3/3/3
    • Myths
      • myth of time : “I don’t have time” is not an excuse
      • myth of motivation
      • myth of multitasking : you can’t do more than one task at the time
    • Laws
      • Parkinson’s law : work expands to fill the time that we allocate to it. (give us artificial deadline) Using calendar to schedule exams, deadlines, studying times etc.
      • Pareto’s principle : (80% of our achievements are made by the 20% useful) Pareto Principle says basically is that 80 percent of the results will come from 20 percent of the effort, or 80 percent of the outputs will result from 20 percent of the inputs in pretty much whatever realm we can think of.
      • 1st Newton’s law : when we start it’s become easier to keep going
    • Powers
      • power of habits or consistency : tiny efforts every day result in big improvements.
      • power of productive downtime : use downtime in your day to do little works or to-do that don’t need much effort and time
      • power of productive procrastinating : doing productive things even when we aren’t working
  • Fun factor
    • when it’s cool and you enjoy what you do, it’s more much easier.
